What Itsaiko does

We plug an AI assistant into your Page’s Messenger to answer FAQs, collect key details, and check availability against your calendar before confirming anything.

  • Polite Taglish tone; short & clear replies
  • “Availability-first” logic, not guesswork
  • Admin can take over anytime
  • No long setup—simple, secure

How we handle data

We don’t keep full conversation history. Message text is processed to generate a reply and (if enabled) check availability. See details in our Privacy Policy.
