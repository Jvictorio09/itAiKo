Itsaiko provides an AI assistant for Facebook Messenger on behalf of businesses (“Clients”). We process messages solely to operate the service.

Data we process

  • Messenger metadata (Page-scoped IDs, webhook events) to route messages.
  • Message text to generate a reply and (if enabled) check calendar availability.
  • Operational logs (timestamps, request IDs, minimal error traces) for reliability & security.

What we don’t do

  • No selling of personal data.
  • No profile-building for advertising.
  • No persistent storage of full conversation history.

Sharing

  • Meta (Facebook) as the messaging platform.
  • AI model provider for generating replies; configured so API data isn’t used for training.
  • Hosting/monitoring providers under strict security and confidentiality.

Retention

Message text is processed transiently to produce a reply. Operational logs are kept for a short period (typically ≤ 30 days) and then deleted.

Security

All endpoints over HTTPS, secrets stored server-side, and least-privilege access controls.

Your rights

Contact the Page you messaged for access/correction/deletion. You can also email us at juliavictorio16@gmail.com and we’ll coordinate with the Page owner.

Children

Not intended for children under 13; we do not knowingly process their data.
